Dog Wizard

    Press...

  • A & D to move left and right
  • W to jump
  • Space to attack
  • Shift to block
  • Arrow keys to shoot fire
  • M to toggle mute
  • P to toggle pause
  • Enter to restart

Allow a minute or two to load on first play
